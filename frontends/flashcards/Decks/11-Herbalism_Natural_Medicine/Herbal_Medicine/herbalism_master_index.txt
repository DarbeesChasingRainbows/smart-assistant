# Herbalism Master Index

## Overview
This comprehensive collection contains 585 questions across complete herbal medicine learning progression: Herbalism Fundamentals (100 questions), Materia Medica (150 questions), Herbal Preparations (150 questions), Clinical Applications (150 questions), Expert Clinical Scenarios (20 questions), and Pediatric Scenarios (15 questions). Organized by systematic learning progression bridging botanical knowledge with clinical practice for professional herbalist certification and healthcare integration across all age groups.

## Certification Alignment
- **AHG (American Herbalists Guild)**: Complete coverage across all levels for registered herbalist certification
- **NCCAOM (National Certification Commission for Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine)**: Herbal medicine component preparation
- **Clinical Herbalist Programs**: Complete foundation for professional herbal practice
- **Integrative Health Practitioner**: Herbal medicine integration with conventional healthcare
- **Traditional Herbalist Guilds**: Traditional knowledge systems and modern practice integration
