Scenario: A multinational corporation experiences a sophisticated ransomware attack with double extortion tactics. The attack has encrypted critical systems across multiple regions, exfiltrated sensitive data, and attackers are threatening public disclosure. The incident response team must coordinate across legal, communications, technical, and business continuity functions.

Question: Which FOUR incident response priorities provide the most effective balance between containment, business continuity, and evidence preservation? (Select ALL that apply)
A) Implement network segmentation and isolation to prevent propagation while preserving forensic evidence
B) Engage legal counsel and regulatory compliance teams for breach notification requirements
C) Establish crisis communications protocol for internal and external stakeholder management
D) Create forensic images of critical systems before any recovery operations begin
E) Evaluate ransom payment options and negotiate with attackers for data recovery
F) Activate business continuity and disaster recovery plans for critical operations

Answer: A, B, C, D

Rationale:
- A) Correct because targeted containment prevents spread while maintaining evidence collection capabilities
- B) Correct because legal and regulatory compliance is critical for avoiding penalties and managing liability
- C) Correct because crisis communications protects reputation and manages stakeholder expectations
- D) Correct because forensic images preserve evidence for investigation while enabling recovery operations
- E) Incorrect paying ransom encourages criminal activity and doesn't guarantee data recovery or non-disclosure
- F) Incorrect while important for recovery, business continuity activation should follow evidence preservation and containment

Scenario: A financial services organization discovers a long-term Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) operation that has been active for over 18 months. The attackers have established multiple persistence mechanisms, exfiltrated sensitive customer data, and created backdoors across the infrastructure. The team must balance thorough investigation with operational security.

Question: Which FOUR investigation and remediation strategies provide the most comprehensive approach while minimizing attacker awareness? (Select ALL that apply)
A) Conduct stealthy investigation using existing monitoring tools to map attacker infrastructure
B) Implement targeted credential rotation and authentication strengthening without alerting attackers
C) Create decoy systems and honeytokens to monitor attacker activity and gather intelligence
D) Prepare coordinated remediation across all affected systems simultaneously
E) Immediately disconnect all internet connectivity to prevent further data exfiltration
F) Engage threat intelligence partners to identify attack attribution and campaign details

Answer: A, B, C, D

Rationale:
- A) Correct because stealthy investigation preserves attacker access patterns and prevents alerting them to discovery
- B) Correct because targeted credential rotation removes attacker access without triggering immediate detection
- C) Correct because decoy systems provide intelligence on attacker TTPs and monitor ongoing activity
- D) Correct because coordinated remediation prevents attackers from adapting to incremental changes
- E) Incorrect immediate disconnection alerts attackers and may cause destructive actions
- F) Incorrect while valuable for attribution, threat intelligence engagement doesn't provide immediate operational benefits
