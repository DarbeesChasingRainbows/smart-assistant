# First Aid Master Index

## Overview
This comprehensive collection contains 1,184 questions across complete first aid and emergency response learning progression: Emergency response fundamentals (244 questions), Medical emergencies & trauma (244 questions), Medical conditions & chronic care (238 questions), Practical applications (220 questions), and Specialized injuries & environmental conditions (238 questions). Organized by medical certification levels for professional emergency responder preparation.

- **EMT-Basic**: Medical emergencies and trauma management focus
- **Wilderness First Aid**: Specialized injuries and environmental conditions
- **CPR/AED Professional**: Emergency response and practical applications
- **Advanced First Aid**: Chronic care and professional development
