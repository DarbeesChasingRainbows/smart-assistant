# Horticulture & Permaculture Flashcard Master Index

## Overview
This comprehensive collection contains complete three-tier learning progression for professional certification and advanced practice in horticulture and permaculture: Basic content (495 foundational questions) + 8 intermediate decks (240 practical questions) + 7 expert-level decks (42 clinical vignette scenarios). Each expert deck uses clinical vignette scenarios with evidence-based, medical-style precision.

## Cross-Reference Matrix

### Concept Integration Map

**Soil Science** connects to:
- Water Management (drainage, irrigation efficiency)
- Plant Pathology (soil-borne diseases)
- Permaculture Design (earthworks, soil building)
- IPM (soil health for pest resistance)

**Plant Identification** connects to:
- Plant Pathology (species-specific disease susceptibility)
- IPM (pest-host relationships)
- Permaculture Design (species selection)
- Propagation (species-specific techniques)

**Water Management** connects to:
- Soil Science (infiltration, water holding capacity)
- Permaculture Design (earthworks, water harvesting)
- Plant Pathology (disease pressure from moisture)
- IPM (pest pressure reduction)

**Propagation Techniques** connects to:
- Plant Identification (species-specific methods)
- Soil Science (media requirements)
- Water Management (mist systems, irrigation)
- Permaculture Design (nursery systems)

## Difficulty Progression Tags

### Expert Level Characteristics
- **Clinical Vignettes**: Complex scenarios with multiple variables
- **Multiple Correct Answers**: Select ALL that apply format
- **Evidence-Based Rationales**: Scientific justification for each option
- **Cross-Domain Integration**: Problems spanning multiple knowledge areas
- **Decision-Making Priority**: Resource allocation and intervention sequencing

### Question Types by Difficulty
- **Diagnostic Scenarios**: Identify problems from symptoms and conditions
- **Intervention Selection**: Choose optimal management strategies
- **Priority Ranking**: Sequence actions by effectiveness or urgency
- **Systems Integration**: Design comprehensive solutions
- **Troubleshooting**: Analyze failed approaches and alternatives

## Zone-Specific Applications

### Cold Climates (Zones 3-6)
- Focus: Season extension, cold hardiness, winter protection
- Key Decks: plant_pathology_expert, propagation_techniques_expert
- Emphasis: Overwintering, spring startup, fall preparation

### Temperate Climates (Zones 7-8)
- Focus: Balanced season management, moderate climate adaptation
- Key Decks: All decks applicable with seasonal modifications
- Emphasis: Integrated systems, year-round production

### Warm/Humid Climates (Zones 9-10)
- Focus: Heat tolerance, disease pressure, water conservation
- Key Decks: plant_pathology_expert, integrated_pest_management_expert
- Emphasis: Summer management, humidity control, tropical adaptations
