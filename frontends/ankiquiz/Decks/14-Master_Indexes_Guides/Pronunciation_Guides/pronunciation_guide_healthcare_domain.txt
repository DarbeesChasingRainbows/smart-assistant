# Healthcare Domain Pronunciation Guide

## Overview
This guide provides phonetic pronunciation for medical terminology, anatomical terms, and physiological concepts used throughout the Healthcare domain collections (Basic Sciences, Anatomy & Physiology, First Aid). Pronunciations use simplified phonetic spelling with emphasis on syllable stress for effective medical communication.

## Usage Notes

1. **Stress Marks**: Capitalized syllables receive primary stress
2. **Syllable Separation**: Hyphens indicate syllable breaks
3. **Medical Accuracy**: Pronunciations follow standard medical terminology conventions
4. **Clinical Context**: Terms organized by body system for clinical relevance
5. **Professional Communication**: Essential for healthcare documentation and patient care

## Study Tips

- **Practice**: Say terms aloud multiple times for mastery
- **Break Down**: Focus on one syllable at a time for complex terms
- **Context**: Learn pronunciations within clinical scenarios
- **Regular Review**: Practice regularly to maintain accuracy
- **Professional Use**: Apply pronunciations in clinical documentation

This pronunciation guide supports effective medical communication and professional development in Healthcare education and clinical practice.
