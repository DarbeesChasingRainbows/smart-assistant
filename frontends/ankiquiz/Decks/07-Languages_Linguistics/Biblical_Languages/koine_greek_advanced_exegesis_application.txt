What is the primary purpose of advanced Greek exegesis in biblical studies?
To draw out the precise meaning of New Testament texts through comprehensive analysis of grammar, syntax, context, and theology for accurate interpretation and application.

What is the difference between grammatical-historical and literary-exegetical approaches?
Grammatical-historical exegesis focuses on original meaning and context, while literary-exegetical emphasizes narrative structure, rhetoric, and theological themes.

What is the primary purpose of Greek syntactical analysis in exegesis?

What is the difference between surface structure and deep structure in Greek syntax?
