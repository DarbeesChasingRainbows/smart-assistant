## üìö Deck Organization & Learning Strategy

### **üéØ Progressive Learning Path (4 Decks)**

#### **Deck 1: Basic Biology Fundamentals & Cell Biology (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Cell structure, function, tissues, organs, and organ systems
- Cell biology: prokaryotic/eukaryotic cells, organelles, cellular processes
- Tissues and organs: epithelial, connective, muscle, nervous tissues
- Organ systems: circulatory, respiratory, digestive, nervous, endocrine systems
- Homeostasis: regulatory mechanisms and feedback systems
- Cellular processes: transport, metabolism, division, and communication

**Learning Outcome**: Master fundamental biology and understand cellular complexity as evidence of God's design

#### **Deck 2: Basic Biology Genetics & Heredity (100 cards)**
**Focus**: DNA, inheritance, genetic variation, and molecular biology
- DNA structure and function: genetic code, replication, transcription, translation
- Inheritance patterns: Mendelian genetics, probability, sex-linked traits
- Cellular division: mitosis, meiosis, and their purposes in God's design
- Gene expression: regulation, epigenetics, and environmental influences
- Applications: genetic testing, biotechnology, and ethical considerations

**Learning Outcome**: Understand genetic mechanisms and variation as part of God's created order

#### **Deck 3: Basic Biology Diversity & Ecology (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Classification, ecosystems, environmental relationships, and stewardship
- Biological classification: kingdoms, species, binomial nomenclature
- Ecosystem dynamics: food webs, energy flow, nutrient cycles
- Environmental relationships: symbiosis, competition, predator-prey dynamics
- Biomes and habitats: terrestrial and aquatic ecosystems
- Conservation: environmental stewardship and biodiversity protection

**Learning Outcome**: Appreciate biological diversity and ecological relationships as God's interconnected creation

#### **Deck 4: Basic Biology Human Biology & Applications (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Human body systems, health, medicine, and practical applications
- Human body systems: detailed study of all major organ systems
- Human health and nutrition: metabolism, diet, exercise, and wellness
- Medical science: disease prevention, treatment, and healthcare
- Applications: biotechnology, public health, and environmental health
- Stewardship: caring for human health as part of God's creation

**Learning Outcome**: Understand human biology and health as special aspects of God's creative work
