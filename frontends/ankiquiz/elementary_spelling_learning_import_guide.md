### **üéØ Progressive Learning Path (6 Decks)**

#### **3rd Grade Level 1 (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Foundational spelling patterns and basic vocabulary
- High-frequency words: about, after, again, along, always
- Common patterns: consonant blends, vowel teams, digraphs
- Basic sight words: does, done, draw, drink, early
- Simple compound words: inside, outside, everything
- Essential verbs and nouns: bring, called, came, clean, cold

**Learning Outcome**: Master basic spelling patterns and high-frequency vocabulary

#### **3rd Grade Level 2 (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Intermediate patterns and longer words
- More complex patterns: beautiful, believe, bought, breakfast
- Multi-syllable words: building, different, dinner, doctor
- Silent letters and tricky spellings: know, knife, write
- Common prefixes and suffixes: unhappy, helpful, quickly
- Academic vocabulary: measure, medicine, mention, minute

**Learning Outcome**: Develop confidence with intermediate spelling challenges

#### **4th Grade Level 1 (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Advanced patterns and academic vocabulary
- Complex multi-syllable words: accident, address, afraid, agree
- Scientific and technical terms: airplane, album, alert, alien
- Academic vocabulary: analysis, ancient, anger, angle
- Common prefixes: un-, re-, pre-, dis-
- Proper nouns and specialized terms: April, August, autumn

**Learning Outcome**: Master advanced elementary spelling and academic vocabulary

#### **4th Grade Level 2 (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Sophisticated patterns and subject-specific vocabulary
- Subject-specific terms: calculator, calendar, camera, camp
- Complex compound words: nevertheless, neighborhood, necessary
- Advanced academic vocabulary: accommodate, accompany, accomplish
- Technical and scientific terms: bacteria, carbon, chemical, chest
- Literary and cultural terms: character, chapter, ceremony, century

**Learning Outcome**: Prepare for middle school with sophisticated vocabulary

#### **5th Grade Level 1 (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Middle school preparation vocabulary
- Advanced academic terms: accommodate, accompany, accomplish
- Scientific and mathematical vocabulary: analysis, ancient, anxiety
- Social studies terms: democracy, demonstrate, department
- Literary and artistic terms: drama, dramatic, draw, dream
- Complex multi-syllable words: extraordinary, excellent, excitement

**Learning Outcome**: Master vocabulary essential for middle school success

#### **5th Grade Level 2 (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Challenging academic and specialized vocabulary
- Sophisticated academic terms: millennium, miscellaneous, necessary
- Scientific and technical terms: pharmaceutical, phenomenon, submarine
- Social and political terms: privilege, professional, questionnaire
- Literary and cultural terms: silhouette, sophisticated, surveillance
- Advanced vocabulary: verification, vulnerable, wardrobe, weapon

**Learning Outcome**: Achieve spelling mastery for advanced academic work
