## üìö Deck Organization & Learning Strategy

### **üéØ Progressive Learning Path (4 Decks)**

#### **Deck 1: Biblical Hebrew Fundamentals & Basic Vocabulary (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Alphabet, basic grammar, essential vocabulary, foundational concepts
- Hebrew alphabet, consonants, vowel points (niqqud), and pronunciation
- Three noun declensions, construct chains, and basic syntax
- Verb roots (shorashim) and basic Qal stem conjugations
- Essential Old Testament vocabulary (500+ words)
- Basic sentence structure and reading skills

**Learning Outcome**: Master Hebrew fundamentals and basic Old Testament reading ability

#### **Deck 2: Biblical Hebrew Grammar & Syntax (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Advanced grammar, verb stems (binyanim), complex constructions
- Seven major verb stems: Qal, Niphal, Piel, Pual, Hiphil, Hophal, Hithpael
- Weak verb classifications and irregular patterns
- Perfect, imperfect, imperative, infinitive constructs and absolutes
- Participles, pronominal suffixes, and advanced syntax
- Hebrew poetry and parallelism structures

**Learning Outcome**: Understand complex Hebrew grammar for Old Testament text analysis

#### **Deck 3: Biblical Hebrew Old Testament & Textual Studies (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Textual criticism, literary analysis, theological themes
- Masoretic Text, Dead Sea Scrolls, and manuscript traditions
- Biblical genres: law, history, poetry, prophecy, wisdom literature
- Major theological themes: covenant, creation, kingdom, messianism
- Historical-cultural background and archaeological context
- Contemporary interpretive methods and approaches

**Learning Outcome**: Read and analyze Old Testament texts with scholarly understanding

#### **Deck 4: Biblical Hebrew Advanced Exegesis & Application (100 cards)**
**Focus**: Exegetical methods, practical applications, contemporary relevance
- Advanced exegetical methods and hermeneutical approaches
- Hebrew in preaching, teaching, and pastoral ministry
- Contemporary scholarship and digital humanities
- Critical methods: feminist, postcolonial, ecological, reader-response
- Global Christianity and cross-cultural applications

**Learning Outcome**: Apply Hebrew knowledge in ministry, scholarship, and contemporary contexts
